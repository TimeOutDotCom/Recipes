Penne Arrabiata

Description

Roman noodles, that are a bit hot. Very Delicious!

Ingredients

  • penne
  • Tomatoes
  • onions
  • garlic
  • chili
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • sugar
  • Basil
  • parmesano

Preparation

  1. Cut the tomatoes and onions into small pieces
  2. finely mince the garlic and the chili
  3. put oil into the pan and start frying the onions, after some time put some sugar and salt into it
  4. put the tomatoes and the garlic in the pan
  5. start cooking 3 liters of water. When the water boils, put the penne in
  6. put the cooked Penne on tsome warm plates and put the sauce on top
