Mead

Description

Mead is a alcoholical beverage, made of honey that was invented by the mighty vikings.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Honey
  • 3 quarts water
  • 1 packet mead yeast
  • Optional Fruit of Herbal Flavoring

Instructions

  1. sanitize all equipment
  2. Mix honey, water and yeast nutrient
  3. place the mixture in a fermentation vessel
  4. cap with water lock and rubber stopper
  5. allow it to ferment for 2-3 weeks
  6. use a siphon to rack the med into a new fermentation vessel
  7. let it ferment for longer than 6 weeks
  8. Bottle the mead
  9. Soak the corks in hot water until pliable, then cork the bottles
  10. Bottle age for at least a month up to 10 years
